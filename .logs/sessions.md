Concretely, a contract that failed AI analysis showed `FAILED` in the API response but was verified stuck at `PENDING` in the actual database forever. Fixed at the root with `ma.mizan.common.persistence.UuidEntity` (`Persistable<UUID>` + `@PostPersist`/`@PostLoad`), retrofitted onto all 6 entities. **User explicitly decided to hold this fix locally rather than push it early** — it's bundled into the Story 3.3 commit (`a88bafd`), not yet on origin.
